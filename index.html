Still Creek Invasive Species

Still Creek is a long stream that flows through Central Burnaby, British Columbia into Burnaby Lake. Still Creek begins north of Central Park in west Burnaby, flows northwest into Vancouver, and then continues north. The 4 most significant tributaries of Still Creek are Chubb Creek, Guichon Creek, Beecher Creek, and Spring Brook.

Due to urbanization and and development in the surrounding area, many parts of Still Creek hare now hidden below the roads and others have been channelized and enclosed, bearing little resemblance to the original stream.
